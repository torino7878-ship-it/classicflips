Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data we collect: Name, email, and payment details provided when registering, bidding, or listing. Payment processing handled securely via Stripe — we never store card details.

Email communications: We use MailerLite to send transactional and marketing emails. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to understand site usage. No personal data is sold to third parties.

Terms of Service

Last updated: July 2026

Platform fee: Standard 4% platform fee on successful sales, or 2.5% for active Pro members. No listing fee applies.

Listings: All information provided must be accurate. Classic Flips reserves the right to remove listings that violate our guidelines or contain false information.

Bids: Winning bids are binding. Failure to complete a purchase may result in account suspension and a dispute fee.

Escrow: All transactions are processed through compliant multi-currency escrow. Funds are released only after delivery is confirmed by both parties.

Liability: Classic Flips is not liable for vehicle condition beyond what is disclosed in the listing. Buyers are encouraged to arrange independent inspections.
