FAQ

Common questions

Can I list from outside the USA? +

Yes. We accept listings from 38 countries including Japan, UK, Germany, Canada, Australia, France, Italy and more. We verify seller identity and title worldwide before any listing goes live.

What is a Flip Score? +

Our AI-powered ROI rating 0–100. It analyzes global market trends, rarity, mileage, condition and 24 months of real auction data. Scores 85+ indicate a strong buy with high flip potential.

How much does it cost to sell? +

Listing is completely free. We charge 4% only when your car sells (2.5% for Pro members). No listing fees, no photography fees, no charge if it does not sell.

What cars can I list? +

JDM icons (Supra, NSX, RX-7, R34, S2000), American muscle (Mustang, Camaro, Chevelle), European classics (Porsche 911, BMW M3, Ferrari, Alfa Romeo) and other collector vehicles generally 15+ years old with documented history.

How is payment handled? +

All transactions use Stripe escrow in USD, CAD, GBP, EUR, AUD and JPY. Buyer funds are held securely until both parties confirm delivery. Currency conversion at live rates with no hidden fees.

Do you handle international shipping? +

Yes. We connect buyers with vetted shipping partners (Montway, uShip, K-Line) offering door-to-door in 38 countries. You receive 2–3 competitive quotes within 24 hours of auction close with full marine insurance.
